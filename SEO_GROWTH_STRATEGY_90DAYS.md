## ✍️ İÇERİK FORMAT STANDARDI

### Her Yazı Zorunlu Elementleri

```markdown
## İçerik Yapısı (WordPress SEO Uyumlu)

1. H1: SEO Title (keyword başta)
2. Quick Verdict Box (bileşen: QuickVerdictBox)
3. Affiliate Disclosure (bileşen: AffiliateDisclosure)  
4. H2: Table of Contents (bileşen: TableOfContents)
5. H2: Intro — What Is [Tool]? (150-200 kelime)
6. H2: Quick Comparison Table (en az 4 araç)
7. H2: [Tool] Pricing 2026 (bileşen: PricingSection)
8. H2: Key Features (bileşen ile ya da H3 listesiyle)
9. H2: Pros & Cons (bileşen: ProsConsSection)
10. H2: Who Is It For? (bileşen: WhoIsItFor)
11. H2: [Tool] vs [Competitor] (kısa, compare page'e link)
12. H2: Our Verdict

14. H2: FAQ (bileşen: FAQSection, min 4 soru)
15. Related Tools (bileşen: RelatedTools)
16. Author Box (bileşen: AuthorBox)
```
